FAQ
Common
Questions.
How does the AI recognition work?
Each cabinet uses multiple HD cameras and deep-learning vision models to identify products as they're taken or returned — no barcodes or weight sensors required. The system continuously improves through over-the-air model updates.
What products can I sell in the cabinet?
Beverages, snacks, fresh food, cosmetics, electronics accessories, and more. If it fits on a shelf and stays within the 32°F–68°F temperature range, the cabinet can sell it. Our team helps configure optimal planograms for your product mix.
What happens if the AI makes a recognition error?
With 99.6% accuracy, errors are rare. When they do occur, our system flags the transaction for review. Customers are never overcharged — disputed charges are resolved automatically through the platform.
What connectivity does the cabinet need?
A 4G cellular connection is built in — no Wi-Fi dependency. The cabinet operates and processes payments even during brief connectivity interruptions, syncing data when the connection restores.
How long does deployment take?
Cabinets arrive pre-configured with your product catalog and payment settings. Typical setup: unbox, position, plug in power, and go live in under an hour. No specialized technicians required.
How does restocking work?
The cloud dashboard tracks real-time inventory and sends predictive restock alerts before products run out. Operators simply open the service door, restock shelves, and the AI automatically updates inventory — no manual scanning.
What support and warranty are included?
All cabinets include hardware warranty, 24/7 remote diagnostics, OTA software updates, and dedicated account support. Our team monitors machine health proactively and can resolve most issues remotely.
