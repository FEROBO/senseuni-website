Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

About SenseUni

SenseUni ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at senseuni.ai. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us through our forms.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at info@senseuni.com

Information We Collect

Form submissions. When you request a demo or send a message, we collect the information you provide: name, email address, company name, region, and your message. This data is processed through Web3Forms, a third-party form service.

Automatic data. Standard web server logs may record your IP address, browser type, referring URL, pages visited, and access timestamps. This is typical infrastructure-level logging, not targeted tracking.

Cookies. We use a small number of essential cookies. See the Cookies section below for details.

We do NOT collect biometric data. Our AI Smart Cabinet processes transactions anonymously — no facial recognition, no biometric identification of any kind. Product selection is detected by computer vision for inventory purposes only.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to demo requests and business inquiries
  • Improve the website experience and content
  • Send relevant product or partnership information — only when you have provided consent or expressed interest by contacting us

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising targeting.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Web3Forms — processes form submissions on our behalf
  • Cloudflare — provides website hosting, CDN, and security services
  • Google Fonts — serves the typefaces used across this website; font requests may be logged by Google

If we add analytics tools or other services in the future, this section will be updated.

Data Retention

Form submission data (name, email, company, message) is retained for business follow-up purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at info@senseuni.com. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Data Security

All data transmitted between your browser and this website is encrypted via HTTPS. We do not store payment information — there is no payment functionality on this website.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your information, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use care when sharing personal information online.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@senseuni.com.

Cookies

This website uses only essential functional cookies. Specifically:

  • senseuni_cookie_accepted — a localStorage entry (not a traditional cookie) that records whether you have acknowledged this banner, so it does not reappear on every page load. No expiry date is set; it persists until you clear browser storage.
  • Cloudflare security cookies — Cloudflare may set short-lived cookies such as __cf_bm for bot protection and security. These are not used for tracking or advertising.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. Loading Google Fonts generates standard HTTP request logs on Google's servers but does not set cookies on your device.

If we introduce analytics tools in the future, this Cookie section will be updated before any such change takes effect.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us at info@senseuni.com and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
